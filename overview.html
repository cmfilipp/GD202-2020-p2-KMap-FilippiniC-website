OVERVIEW

The level of commitment Colleges and Universities have adopted toward sustainability initiatives on their campuses is large. However, findings indicate a general lack of success toward motivating students to adopt more sustainable behaviors. Sustainability cannot be successful without college students’ engagement. It is crucial that we understand the importance of sustainble actions and thus, interventions that could encourage change.

The goal: As a college student at NC State University, and in recognizing the importance of environmental protection, I have conducted research questioning the awareness of sustainable practices on our campus. The goal of my project is to examine pro-environmental behavior on campus and bring awareness to fellow students.
